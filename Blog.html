My new Job (03/23)

I've been looking for a job for a couple months now and I finally got one, at King Gyros. I had been there a month ago asking if they were hiring and they said yes and told me to give them my phone number and that they would call when they needed me. They never called me so I had thought they just didn't want me to work there but then today they called me saying that I was hired. I was so excited because this is my first formal job ever. I used to have one back in Mexico a few years ago but I only worked there for two weeks and it was something not very formal so. I'll keep updating with new stuff that happens.
