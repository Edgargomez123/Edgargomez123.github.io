Recipe #1 Chicken salad

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: Cool
Ingredients Quantity
Cooked chicken (chopped) 2 cups
Green onions (diced) 1
celery (chopped) 1 stalk
dijon mustard 1 teaspoon
Seasoned salt 1/2 teaspoon
Fresh dill 1 teaspoon
Pepper to taste As much as you want

Step 1: Combine all ingredients in a small bowl and mix well.

Step 2: Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 3: Serve as a sandwich or over salad.Serve as a sandwich or over salad.

Recipe #2 Chicken noodle soup

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: Hot/Warm
Ingredients Quantity
Olive oil 1 teaspoon
Onions (diced) 1
Large carrots (diced) 2
Large celery ribs 2
Red pepper 1
Poultry seasoning 1 teaspoon
Pepper to taste As much as you want
bay leaf 1
chopped cooked chicken 2 cups
Chicken broth 6 cups
Egg noodles 6 oz.
Broccoli florets 1 cup
Table spoons fresh parsley 2-3

Step 1: Heat olive oil in a pot over medium high heat.

Step 2: Chop the onion and add to the pot. Continue adding veggies to the pot in order as you chop them except broccoli.

Step 3: Cook until onion is softened, about 5 minutes.

Step 4: Stir in seasoning, broth and chicken. Bring to a boil, add noodles reduce heat to medium-high and cook until noodles are tender. Add in broccoli during the last 2 minutes of cooking.

Step 5: Stir in parsley and remove bay leaf before serving.

Source: Home made Chicken noodle soup
